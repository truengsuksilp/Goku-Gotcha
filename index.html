Game Description

Hit play to start playing Your job is to keep leveling up through the GA Bootcamp as Goku.

As you go through your GA Bootcamp, your level of hunger / sleep-deprived / boredom will increase. Reaching level 10 on these issues and you die.

To not die, you must do "human things":

  • Eat
  • Sleep
  • Play
